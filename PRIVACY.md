# Grablio Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 10, 2026

## Overview
Grablio is a browser extension that downloads images from web pages. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
Grablio does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

All image scanning, selection, and ZIP packaging happens entirely within your browser. No images, URLs, browsing data, or personal information is ever sent to any external server.

## Local Storage
Grablio uses browser local storage solely to remember your preferences (theme, font, corner radius, session mode) across popup opens. This data never leaves your device.

## Contact
If you have questions, open an issue at https://github.com/elliotalien/Grablio/issues
